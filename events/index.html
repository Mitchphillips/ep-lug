EP-LUG Expo 2019

EPLUG's first official independent exhibit took place in Tataháza with twelve members displaying MOCs in the event hall. We're thankful for the enthusiastic crowd, and can't wait to repeat this meetup next year.

Bio-Cup 2017

2017 marked the return of the Bio-Cup, a moc-pages era contest now revived to spark engagment in Bionicle building and foster a sense of community. With almost 40 builders competing and over 100 MOCs build for it, the return of the Bio-Cup was a tremendous success.
